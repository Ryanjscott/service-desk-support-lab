# Sample Asset Register

## Purpose
This sample asset register demonstrates basic asset tracking for end-user devices in a Level 1 support environment.

| Asset ID | Device Type | Model | Assigned User | Department | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| LAP-001 | Laptop | Dell Latitude 5420 | Sample User 1 | Finance | Active | Standard Windows 11 device |
| LAP-002 | Laptop | HP EliteBook 840 | Sample User 2 | Operations | Active | Microsoft 365 configured |
| MOB-001 | Mobile Phone | iPhone 13 | Sample User 3 | Management | Active | MFA enabled |
| PRN-001 | Printer | Brother MFC-L8900 | Shared | Office | Active | Shared office printer |
| MON-001 | Monitor | Dell 24 inch | Sample User 1 | Finance | Active | Dual monitor setup |
| LAP-003 | Laptop | Lenovo ThinkPad T14 | New Starter | Support | Prepared | Ready for onboarding |
| HDD-001 | Storage Device | SSD 512GB | Unassigned | ICT | Retired | Awaiting secure disposal |

## Asset Tracking Notes
- Asset records should be updated when devices are assigned, moved, repaired, replaced or retired.
- Devices should be tagged before being issued to users.
- Lost, damaged or retired assets should be recorded clearly.
- Asset records should avoid unnecessary personal information.
